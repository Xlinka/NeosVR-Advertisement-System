About the NeosVR Advertisement System

The NeosVR Advertisement System is a platform designed to facilitate advertising within the NeosVR metaverse platform. It allows businesses and individuals to promote their products, services, or events to the NeosVR community through engaging and immersive advertisements.

Reach your target audience and captivate them with interactive and visually stunning ads.

Supporting the Utility of Neos Credits

The NeosVR Advertisement System plays a vital role in enhancing the utility of Neos Credits within the NeosVR metaverse. By leveraging the power of advertisements, businesses and individuals can effectively promote their products, services, or events and encourage transactions using Neos Credits.

Maximize the value of your Neos Credits by driving engagement and generating sales through strategic advertising campaigns.

Efficient Tracking and Management

The NeosVR Advertisement System offers efficient tracking of impressions and clicks for advertisements. With advanced analytics, it provides detailed insights into the performance of your ads, allowing you to measure their effectiveness and make data-driven decisions for your campaigns.

Stay informed about the success of your advertising efforts and optimize your campaigns effortlessly with our intuitive management panel.

Custom Tagging for SFW and NSFW Ads

The NeosVR Advertisement System provides a flexible tagging system that allows businesses and individuals to categorize their advertisements as either SFW or NSFW. This feature ensures that appropriate content is displayed to users based on their preferences and the context of the world they are in.

In addition, the system respects user privacy and world settings. NSFW advertisements are only shown in hidden and private worlds, where the environment is explicitly set to allow such content. This approach maintains a safe and respectful environment while providing advertisers with targeted visibility in suitable contexts.

Review and Payment

Every submitted ad will undergo a thorough review process before going live on the advertisement system. Once confirmed, the ad will turn green and be displayed to the NeosVR community. Ads under review will appear as yellow in the management panel, while ads that have been denied will be shown in red.

Please note that payment is taken before the review process due to the nature of cryptocurrency transactions. Ensure that your advertisement complies with both our guidelines and NeosVR guidelines to guarantee a smooth and appropriate advertising experience.
